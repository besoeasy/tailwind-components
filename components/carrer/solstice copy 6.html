What we’re building

People are shifting to a new form of collaboration in distributed teams and are wasting too much effort connecting the dots between different work streams to understand the bigger picture. Solstice is building a work hub that gives people a radically simpler way to coordinate work across teams and get context on what’s happening.

By bringing structure, access, and efficiency to teamwork. Backed by Atomico, Salesforce Ventures, and Mosaic, we’re on a mission to unlock the collective potential of teams and would love for you to join us!
